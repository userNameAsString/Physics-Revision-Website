Power Equations

Power is defined as the rate at which energy is transferred or the rate at which work is done.

Power is measured in watts(W) which just means joules per second.

The power output of an object can be calculated using either of the equations:

power= energy transferred/time taken

P = E/t

energy transferred, E, in joules, J

power= work done/time taken

P = W/t

work done, W, in joules, J

recall and apply

power, P, in watts, W

time, t, in seconds, s

Note: Both equations can be used to calculate power and both are recall and apply

Rearrange to find:

energy - E = Pt

time - t = P/E
