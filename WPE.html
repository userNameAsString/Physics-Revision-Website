Wave Period Equation

The period of a wave is how long it takes a ONE whole wavelength to pass a point.

The period of a wave can be calculated using the equation:

period = 1/frequency

T = 1/f

select and apply

period, T, in seconds, s

frequency, f, in hertz, Hz

Rearrange to find:

frequency - f = f = 1/T
