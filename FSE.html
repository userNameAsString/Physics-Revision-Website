Final Suvat Equation

Suvat refers to the symbols for distance(s), initial velocity(u), final velocity(v), acceleration(a) and time(t)

Both the average acceleration equation and the constant speed equations are also suvat equations

This suvat equation links, distance(s), initial velocity(u), final velocity(v) and acceleration(a)

final velocity2 - initial velocity2 = 2 x acceleration x distance

v2 - u2 = 2as

select and apply

final speed, v, in metres per second, m/s

starting speed, u, in metres per second, m/s

acceleration, a, in metres per second squared, m/s2

distance, d, in metres, m

Rearrange to find:

final speed - v = √u2 - 2as

starting speed - u = √v2 - 2as

acceleration - a = v2 - u2 / 2s

distance - s = v2 - u2 / 2a
