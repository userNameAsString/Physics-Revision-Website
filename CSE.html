Constant Speed Equation

The constant speed of an object is how fast it moves without going faster or slower.

The constant speed of an object can be calculated using the equation:

distance = speed * time

s = vt

recall and apply

distance, s, in metres, m

speed, v, in metres per second, m/s

time, t, in seconds, s

Rearrange to find:

speed - v = s/t

time - t = s/v
